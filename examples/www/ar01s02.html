Note

Headings can be automatically numbered with by adding the :sectnums: attribute to the document header. However, autonumbering of headings don’t appear to carry through to docbook output and downstream files, e.g. chunked xhtml. Automatic links and anchors can be enabled with the :sectlinks: and :sectanchors: attributes.
